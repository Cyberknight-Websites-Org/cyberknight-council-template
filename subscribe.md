## What You'll Receive:

### Monthly Newsletters
Monthly email newsletters featuring our meeting schedule, upcoming events, recent website posts, and a link to the newest Columbia magazine issue.

### Announcement Emails
Be aware about upcoming council events, calls for help, and be more in the loop of what's happening.

### Announcement Texts
Infrequent texts such as reminders for events happening the next day, as well as urgent calls for help.
